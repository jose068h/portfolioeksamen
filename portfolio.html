About the Project

This project was coding my first responsive website. I was assigned the style "modern futeriatic" and I made a website about Singapore, Gardens by The Bay. The whole area is very modern and futuristic which is reflected in the design and architecture of the gardens. Therefore, I found it obvious to make a modern and futuristic website on this topic. The website contains 6 sections, primarily information and a gallery.

Manu Sareen - A passion for humanity

Previous minister for Children, Gender Equality, Integration and Social Affairs. Now Author, Coach and TV-host.

Manu Sareen has had a passion for people all his life. Manu has a great injustice gene, if he experiences something that is unfair he has a huge drive to take action. It has been one of many prerequisites for ending up in Danish politics. Manu is currently writing a new book, is a TV host on his show "Men and Women are from Earth", is going to start YouTube and is on a lecture tour with Christine Feldthaus, where they talk about love, sex and single culture. Manu has also written the bestselling books "Iqbal Farooq" written about the Danish-Indian Nørrebro boy Iqbal Farooq in a humorous tone that touches on themes such as integration, cultural encounters and diversity.
